# Covenantal Media AI – Agentic AI Television Studio  
### Open‑Source Multi‑Agent Framework for AI‑Driven Television Production

Covenantal Media AI PBC is building an open‑source, agentic AI television production framework designed to demonstrate how modern AI tools can dramatically reduce the cost of high‑quality film and media creation.

Only a few years ago, producing a three‑minute cinematic trailer required large budgets, professional crews, physical sets, and extensive equipment. Today, AI text‑to‑film platforms make it possible for independent creators to produce Hollywood‑level visuals at a fraction of the cost. This technological shift represents a new era in global media accessibility and economic opportunity.

Covenantal Media AI PBC is working to redirect these massive production cost savings toward a new purpose: **making humanoid embodied intelligence affordable and accessible for ordinary people.** Our long‑term model uses advertising‑supported AI‑generated media to subsidize extended repair, maintenance, and replacement services for humanoid robots beyond limited manufacturer warranties. This approach aims to keep robots operational in real‑world environments for longer lifespans, reducing total cost of ownership and accelerating global adoption.

As part of this mission, we are developing an **AI Television Production Agent** as a proof‑of‑concept for a future AI‑driven cable television network focused on entrepreneurship and economic development. This proof‑of‑concept is being built inside **Alibaba Cloud** using **Qwen models**, Model Studio, and GPU compute, with the goal of establishing a scalable production workflow suitable for a future B2B partnership with **Mango Television** for U.S. market distribution.

In parallel, Covenantal Media AI PBC is negotiating wholesale pricing and Manufacturer Letters of Intent with leading humanoid robotics companies including **UBTECH**, **Fourier Intelligence**, **Agility Robotics**, and **Unitree**. We have also onboarded high‑ticket sales professionals in **Bahrain**, **Peru**, and **Brazil** who are working to place humanoid robots in luxury resorts worldwide.

This repository contains the **open‑source agentic AI television studio** used to prototype this vision. All system prompts, agent definitions, and workflow logic are published here for transparency, collaboration, and global inspection.
